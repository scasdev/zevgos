Tired of running tedious pair-trading calculations? You’re in luck, Zevgos was created for the crypto day trader like yourself.

Find your coins!

With the Zevgos Crypto Pair-trading app, you can easily enter two co-integrated crypto currencies, along with the currency they would like to see the pair-trading formulas applied to. Once you select the "Generate Pair Data" button, the app will pull the most recent price data, run the numbers, and present you with some useful info on each coin.

View the data!

Just scroll down enter your co-integrated pairs. You’ll see the "ratio", the "formula", the “spread”, and much more.
