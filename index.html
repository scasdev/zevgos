Tired of running tedious pair-trading calculations? You’re in luck, Zevgos was created for crypto day traders like you.

Find your coins!

With the Zevgos Crypto Pair-trading app, you can enter two co-integrated crypto currencies, the currency you would like to see the pair-trading formulas applied to, and once you select the "Generate Pair Data" button, the app will do a few things.

  1. Pull the most recent price data
  2. Run the numbers
  3. Present you with some useful info on each coin

View the data!

Just scroll down enter your co-integrated pairs. You’ll see the "ratio", the "formula", the “spread”, and much more.
